How Heroes Work

Hero Creation

When starting the game, you choose a Race and Class. Each combination produces a unique hero with different stats, abilities, and racial traits. There are 6 races and 4 classes, giving 24 standard heroes plus 1 secret unlock.

Stats System

Each hero's stats are derived from a base template modified by both race and class bonuses. This means two warriors of different races will play quite differently.

Abilities

Each class has 4 abilities shared across all races of that class. Abilities cost mana and range from passive bonuses to devastating ultimates. Class determines your playstyle.

  • Warrior: Tank / melee DPS with Avatar ultimate
  • Worg: Shapeshifter with multiple combat forms
  • Mage: Ranged caster with AoE and healing
  • Ranger: Ranged physical DPS with stealth

Racial Traits

Each race provides 2 passive traits that apply at all times. These stack with class abilities and can create powerful synergies (e.g., Orc Bloodrage + Worg Feral Rage).

Factions

Heroes belong to one of 3 factions which determines their visual theme and story alignment:

  • Crusade - Human, Barbarian (+ Pirate King)
  • Fabled - Dwarf, Elf
  • Legion - Orc, Undead

Rarity & Difficulty

Rarity reflects how unique the hero's playstyle is. Difficulty rates how hard the hero is to master:

  • Common / Uncommon - Standard heroes
  • Rare / Epic - Unique mechanics
  • Legendary - Top-tier complexity

Secret Hero

Creating a Barbarian Ranger named exactly "Racalvin the Pirate King" unlocks a secret Legendary hero with boosted stats (+30 HP, +5 ATK, +5 SPD) and a unique ultimate ability: Storm of Arrows.
